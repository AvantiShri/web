Selected Publications

arXiv
Jan 2019		 Calibration with Bias-Corrected Temperature Scaling Improves Domain Adaptation Under Label Shift in Modern Neural Networks.
Avanti Shrikumar†,Anshul Kundaje†.
† co-corresponding authors

Proposed a modification of Temperature Scaling that corrects for systematic biases in the calibrated probabilities. This allows domain adaptation under label shift to be performed using an EM algorithm, outperforming the recently-proposed Black Box Shift Estimation (ICML 2018).

arXiv
July 2018		 Computationally Efficient Measures of Internal Neuron Importance.
Avanti Shrikumar*†, Jocelin Su*, Anshul Kundaje†.
*co-first authors
† co-corresponding authors

Showed an equivalence between Total Conductance, a recently-proposed method for computing internal neuron importance, and Path Intergrated Gradients, thereby providing a computationally efficient way to compute the former.
